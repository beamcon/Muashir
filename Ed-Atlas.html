About Educational Atlas

The educational atlas is one of the national repositories of health, educational and social care data that is collected from raw and secondary data for the Saudi population data set in a regional manner. Statistics, figures and reliable information about education, its development and the availability of its facilities for the population of the Kingdom. It aims to improve the quality of information and data and to benefit from that rich information in analyzing the situation of education and developing its policies, and how to implement educational policies in the educational field. It contains a number of specialized reports and indicators in the field of education and training in the Kingdom.

Data content

The platform is a major reference for researchers, interested parties, stakeholders and the general public. Through an analytical window and a presentation of a set of available data and information sets related to the state of education in the Kingdom, and the presentation of indicators related to the fields of education, as it supports raising the quality of education in the public and private sectors, their adequacy, and their contribution to serving the economy and national development of the Kingdom.
(Demography, educational staff, type and distribution of educational facilities in the Kingdom, educational and administrative offices in the education sector, scholarship program, educational administrations, geospatial analyzes and presentations).

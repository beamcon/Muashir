About Muashir

An indicator is a set of interactive geographic information system-based dashboards that includes illustrations such as charts, maps, charts, or text materials. For policy makers, analysts, researchers, the public, and investors. It can be a multi-platform digital Atlas system based on web or applications (IOS, Android). The Health Atlas and Education Atlas are implemented under the Digital Atlas project of BIM Consulting and Research and the contributions of the project partners. This site is still under development and is subject to change.

Purpose of Muashir

1. Support evidence-based policy planning and implementation and comprehensive and inclusive learning and decision-making processes in Saudi Arabia by providing rich online, highly interactive and dynamic social, health and education data with standard preprocessing and basic data analysis tools
2. Aggregate single source data from a variety of areas needed for effective policy design and targeting.
3. Ensure broad access to high-quality, disaggregated data, administrative regions, and social classes to facilitate comprehensive review and dialogue processes.
